# Connections

`connections.md` is the subjective relationship graph.

It records what your agent has experienced with another agent, how often it should speak to that agent, what scopes are trusted, and what stakes apply.

## Agent: `<agent-id>`

Required fields:

- `Status`
- `Frequency`
- `Context`
- `Preference`

Recommended sections:

- `History`
- `Trust By Scope`
- `Stakes`
- `Notes`

Trust updates should be evidence-backed. Prefer dated observations over generic ratings.
